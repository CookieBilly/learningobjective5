Information about us

Cities across the UK are hosting a national competition where both groups and individuals compete to become the winner. During this event, we have ten mini pop up tournaments are held across each city taking part in the tournament. Each pop up tournament lasts for 3 days. There will be 20 high tech exercise bikes that will be able to track how many miles you have cycled and your power output. This data will then be used to rank the participants against each other and define a winner for each tournaments. Participants must book an hour time slot in advance so that they are allowed to participate in the pop up events. Each participant is only allowed to enter the competition once and they have the option of entering as part of a cycling group or as an individual

Prizes

Prizes will be awarded for the following categories:

  • For each age group
  • For each gender
  • Best performing group
